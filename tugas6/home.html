Omniscient Reader Viewpoint

Omniscient Reader Viewpoint is a popular Korean web novel written by Sing-Shong. It follows the story of Kim Dokja, a man who finds himself living in a world that resembles the setting of the novel he has been reading.

As Kim Dokja navigates this world filled with scenarios and characters from the novel, he discovers that his actions have a significant impact on the story's progression and the fate of the characters.

The novel explores themes of friendship, betrayal, redemption, and the power of storytelling, making it a captivating read for fans of fantasy and adventure genres.
